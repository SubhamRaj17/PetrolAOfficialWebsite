Welcome to Petrol Aficionados

Founded in 2021, the Petrol Aficionados started hosting races on Assetto Corsa Competizione (ACC). Later on, our focus shifted to our own racing team instead. Now, with multiple endurance events under our belt and some solid finishes, we're continuing to improve. Be it more races we participate in, more races we host ourselves, or better finishes we get, PetrolA Racing is not going anywhere anytime soon.

With unhinged humor, stunning liveries, and only the highest quality spins, we're constantly looking to dabble into different areas of simracing. If you'd like to keep up-to-date with what we do or what we're planning on doing, feel free to join our discord or follow our social media hannels. Who knows, maybe you'll get a spot with us too; we'd love to have you onboard for the journey.
